DIAMONDS

Diamond is a solid form of the element carbon with its atoms arranged in a crystal structure called diamond cubic.

Types of Diamonds

Round

The round brilliant cut is the most popular and classic shape for diamonds. It features 58 facets, maximizing brilliance and sparkle.

Princess

The princess cut is a square or rectangular-shaped diamond with pointed corners. It offers a modern and elegant appearance.

Emerald

The emerald cut has a rectangular shape with cropped corners. It emphasizes clarity over brilliance, showcasing the diamond's natural beauty.

Marquise

The marquise cut is an elongated diamond with pointed ends. It creates the illusion of a larger diamond due to its elongated shape.
